WHAT THE PHO?

Pho (pronounced “fuh”) is a traditional Vietnamese noodle soup. It starts with a carefully seasoned beef broth that is slow-simmered overnight. The broth is ladled over thin, flat rice noodles and then topped with meats and garnishes. Pho can be eaten any time of day – just don’t forget the housin sauce & Sriracha!
